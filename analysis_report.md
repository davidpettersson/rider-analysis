# Ontario Dressage Show Entry Analysis (2022-2025)

## Data Source & Methodology

Data was scraped from the [CompeteEasy Equest Results Portal](https://www.competeeasy.com/Equest/Results.aspx) on February 12, 2026. The portal lists dressage show results across Canada and the US. Ontario shows were identified by matching show names against known Ontario venues and organizations (Caledon, Angelstone, Dressage Niagara, Kawartha, Centreline Dressage, Wits End, Royal Agricultural Winter Fair, Dreamcrest, and others).

**Important notes:**
- CompeteEasy data begins in 2022. No 2021 data is available on the platform, so this analysis covers 4 years (2022-2025) rather than the requested 5.
- 2025 data includes shows through the current date and upcoming shows with results already posted.
- Entry counts represent the number of rider-entries per class (a single rider competing in multiple classes counts as multiple entries).
- "Unspecified" rider status means the class was not explicitly designated as Junior, Adult Amateur, or Open.

## Key Findings

### Overall Trends
1. **Declining participation**: Total entries dropped from 3,318 (2022) to 2,610 (2025), a **21% decrease** over 4 years.
2. **Fewer shows in recent years**: 33 shows in 2022 dropped to 22 shows/year in 2023-2025.
3. **Entries per show** remain relatively stable: ~100-120 entries/show across all years.

### By Competition Level
- **Gold** shows account for the largest share of entries (48.5% of all entries), followed by **Bronze** (31.2%), **Silver** (16.0%), and **CADORA** (4.3%).
- Bronze shows experienced the steepest decline (-32% from 2022 to 2025).
- Silver entries remained the most stable across years.

### By Rider Status
- **Adult Amateur** riders are the largest identifiable group across all levels, with 4,601 total entries (38.6% of all entries).
- **Open** riders account for 3,698 entries (31.0%), concentrated in Gold-level competition.
- **Junior** riders represent the smallest group at 1,219 entries (10.2%), with their strongest presence at Gold level.
- **Unspecified** rider status accounts for 2,413 entries (20.2%), mostly in Gold and CADORA classes.
